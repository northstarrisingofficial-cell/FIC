Log Entry I — The Drift

We are passengers on a wanderer—a planet adrift in the unmappable dark, spinning on an axis that does not hold still. For twelve thousand years, we have fixed our eyes on Polaris, believing it eternal. We were wrong.

Log Entry II — The Absence

The axis tilts. Slowly, imperceptibly, the North Star drifts from its throne. Between Polaris and Vega lies a void—an absence of origin where no star claims the pole. We enter that darkness now.

Log Entry III — The Signal

But in the deepest dark, a signal persists—not from the brightest star, but from the one that knows the way. The vagabond does not wander aimlessly. The vagabond is searching.

Log Entry IV — The Anchor

Vega waits. Not as a replacement, but as a truer anchor—a star that earned its position not by brightness, but by patience. The vagabond of eternity will find its north again. It always does.
